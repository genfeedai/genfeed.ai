# Organizations

Organizations are the tenant boundary in Genfeed Cloud. They group brands,
members, roles, and commercial state.

Cloud supports multiple isolated organizations. Community intentionally creates
one organization per deployment and does not expose multi-tenant organization
switching as a product surface.

Authentication organization records are a session bridge; Genfeed's own
organization, member, and role records remain the domain authorization source.
See [Execution Boundaries](/core/execution-boundaries) for the Cloud/Community
split.
