# Team and Member Governance

Team and multi-organization governance are Cloud/Enterprise product surfaces.
The repository contains organization, member, and role domain models plus
authorization infrastructure, but exact role names, permissions, invitation
flows, seat terms, and limits depend on the deployed release and commercial
entitlement.

Community remains one organization per deployment. Enabling its local Better
Auth login wall provides local accounts; it does not turn the Community image
into the commercial multi-tenant distribution.

Use the current product UI and pricing terms as the source of truth. This page
does not promise a seat count, role matrix, support channel, or SLA.
