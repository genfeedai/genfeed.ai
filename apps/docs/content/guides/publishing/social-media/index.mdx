# Social Media Setup

Connect your social accounts and publish AI-generated content directly from Genfeed.ai. Each platform has its own authentication and optimization rules — the guides below cover setup, requirements, and best practices per platform.

## Platform Guides

- **[YouTube](/guides/publishing/social-media/youtube)** — Long-form videos, Shorts, and live streams.
- **[Instagram](/guides/publishing/social-media/instagram)** — Posts, Reels, Stories, and carousels.
- **[TikTok](/guides/publishing/social-media/tiktok)** — Short-form videos and trending content.
- **[LinkedIn](/guides/publishing/social-media/linkedin)** — Professional content and thought leadership.

## How Connecting Works

1. Navigate to **Accounts** → **Add Account**.
2. Select the platform and complete its OAuth flow — Genfeed never stores your password.
3. Once connected, the account is available as a publishing target across the Studio and Workflows.
